ABOUT US

The school was opened to facilitate and offer quality learning in, around and beyond my village with a high-state vision to also spot, nurture and support children’s talents in class and co curricular activities. We are an Education and Talent supportive institution

St. Martins de Porres started in 2012 as a Primary Day-Only School project purposefully to help the community but gradually became a Primary Day and Boarding school. It is located 800 m off Tala-Kisukioni road and 2km from Nguluni town.
